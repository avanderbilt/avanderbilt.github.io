## Jesus Prayer

### In English

Lord Jesus Christ, Son of God, have mercy on me a sinner.

### In Russian

Господи Иисусе Христе, Сыне Божий, помилуй мя грешнаго.

#### Transliteration

Gospodi Iisuse Khriste, Syne Bozhiy, pomiluy mya greshnago.
